MCU Phase 1 Ranked

Have you watched all the movies in Phase 1 of the MCU? Well I have and I want to make this website about the rankings that I would put each movie in and why. This is very opinonated website, because when it comes to things that are generally consumed it would be such thing. In the follwing of the website I want to show you the rankings that I have put the first full phase of the MCU under some ratings that I came up with on my own, and put them in the best order possible due to how they fell in those rankings.

Here is the list:

This is what is taken into account for each score...
